Broccoli

Description

Broccoli was grown in the Roman Empire, and was likely improved via artifical selection in Italy. Broccoli
spread to Northern Europe in the 18th and brought to North America in the 19th century.Some of
the cultivars that have been most popular are the Premium Crop,Packman and Marathon.
